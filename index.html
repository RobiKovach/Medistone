WHAT IS MEDISTONE?

Medistone is the only boutique hotel on the beach in Medici on the Omis riviera. It's a great location for couples and families who love the easy-going nature of Dalmatian places, crystal-clear sea and pebble beaches. Hotel Medistone is a place where you can spend hours lounging on the beach or taking a nap with the sound of the waves in the apartments overlooking the sea. If you crave tasty food, our hotel offers delicious, freshly prepared fish and meat dishes, and if you want to relax, choose the wellness zone or the privacy of the pool or a jacuzzi.
