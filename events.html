Weddings

You can book a restaurant or the entire hotel for all of your guests. Amazing special menu, beach for photo shooting or wedding ceremony, spa for the day after…And there are only a few advantages of making the wedding at Medistone. And yes, on the wedding day the bride and the groom sleep for free.

Celebrations

Our chef and his team will organise your best banquet (European or Dalmatian style). We have some MENU&BAR offers for your celebration, so you can choose according to your budget and taste. If you need we can help you with party organising: decorations, animators, candy bar and cake, balloons, live music and so on.

Kids party

It’s a brilliant idea to celebrate kids party at Medistone. We can organise two zones: for kids and for adults. We also can help with some party decorations or animators. And of course we have kids menu: children say they really love our pizza, burgers and nuggets.
