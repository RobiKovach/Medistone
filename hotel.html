About Us

Hotel Medistone is located in Medići, close to the town of Omiš. Medistone is new and stylish boutique-hotel with “Art of relaxation” concept. We have private parking, spa area and wellness procedures, amazing sea view restaurant with terrace, gym, water attractions and much more services for your best stay.

Great Location

The resort is located at an approximately 10-minute drive from Omiš, a popular tourist town located at the mouth of the Cetina river in the Adriatic Sea. Omiš Riviera with apartments by the sea and pebble beaches. It is most appealing to couples and families with children who want to relax in the shade and spend their vacation in Croatia without overcrowding.

free Parking

Guests of the Medistone Hotel have 20 parking spaces available on the roof of the hotel. The entrance to the hotel parking lot is directly from the Adriatic Highway (D8) – on the right side when coming from the direction of Split or on the left side if you are coming from the direction of Šestanovac and Makarska.

ELectric charger

For guests traveling by e-cars we have free electric charger at our parking. Also you can bring your e-bikes or get ours for rent – this charger is available for them also.
